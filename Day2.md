What I understood:
Defensive security focuses on protecting systems and devices from attacks.

Before defending a system, it is important to understand the responsibilities of a defender. Defensive security involves detecting, analyzing, and responding to cyber threats before they cause damage.

Unlike offensive security, defensive security does not involve attacking systems. Instead, it focuses on monitoring, preventing, and securing systems.
