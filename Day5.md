# Day 5 - Cybersecurity Journey

Today I learned more about networking concepts and how attacks happen in networks.

Topics covered:
- Types of networks (LAN, MAN, WAN, PAN)
- Network devices and fiber optic cables
- Packet sniffing and network analysis
- Protocol exploitation
- Network reconnaissance
- Firewall evasion

What I understood:
Networks can be categorized based on their size and usage. Different devices and cables are used to connect systems and transfer data.

I also learned that attackers can exploit networks using techniques like packet sniffing, reconnaissance, and firewall evasion to gather information or bypass security.
